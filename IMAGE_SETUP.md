# Image Setup Instructions

Place your files in the following locations inside the `public/` folder:

## Logo
public/
  images/
    logo.png          ← Your file: logo_.png

## Product Images
public/
  images/
    products/
      huile-cheveux-1.jpg   ← IMG_3091_1.jpg  (3 bottles branded wall)
      huile-cheveux-2.jpg   ← IMG_3091_2.jpg  (2 bottles + botanicals)
      huile-cheveux-3.jpg   ← IMG_3091_3.jpg  (luxury box)
      huile-cheveux-4.jpg   ← IMG_3091.jpg    (single bottle on gold stand)

## Gallery (optional — for future images)
public/
  images/
    gallery/
      gallery-5.jpg   ← add more Instagram-style lifestyle shots here
      gallery-6.jpg

## Notes
- The product images are used in: homepage hero, featured products, PDP, Instagram gallery
- Optimal size: 800×1000px (3:4 ratio) for product shots, max 200KB (compress with squoosh.app)
- Logo will appear in navbar and footer — use the PNG with black background as-is
